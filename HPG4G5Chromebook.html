HP G4/G5 Chromebook

This section goes into detail about the HP G4 and G5 Chromebooks, including how to repair their hardware components.

The HP G4/G5 Chromebook was/is issued to the classes of 2020 onward. Providing HP does not make major design changes to the G6 Chromebook, this section can also apply to them.


General consensus has it that the HP Chromebooks are more nicely built than their Dell counterparts. However, their I/O is not in the right place (but it is important to count one's blessings as actual I/O is not a given anymore these days), they are a pain to repair, their manufacturer is irritating, but they have four year warranties so we only repair LCDs.

Specs:

Processor (G4): Intel Celeron N2840 @ 2.16 GHz x 2 cores

Processor (G5): Intel Celeron N3060 @1.6 GHz x 2 Cores

Graphics: Integrated Intel HD Graphics

RAM: 2GB DDR3L, soldered to motherboard

Storage: 16 GB eMMC flash storage, soldered to motherboard

Display: 11.6 inch, 1366x768 TN LCD Display

I/O: HP Power, 2x USB 3.0, HDMI, 3.5mm audio
